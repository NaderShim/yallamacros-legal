About YallaMacros

YallaMacros is a macro tracker built for Dubai and Gulf expats. The app helps you set personalized protein, carbohydrate, and fat targets, log what you eat from a regional food database covering Arab, South Asian, Filipino, and Western cuisines, and track your progress week by week.

Frequently asked questions

How do I cancel my subscription?
Subscriptions are managed by Apple. Open the iOS Settings app, tap your name at the top, then Subscriptions, find YallaMacros, and tap Cancel Subscription. You can also tap Manage subscription in the app's Account section to jump straight there.
How do I get a refund?
Apple handles all refunds for App Store purchases. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com, sign in with your Apple ID, and request a refund for your YallaMacros purchase.
How do I delete my account?
Open the app, scroll to the Account section on the Today screen, and tap Delete account. This permanently removes your profile, meal plans, weight history, and meal logs from our servers. If you have an active subscription, remember to cancel it separately via iOS Settings.
How is my data used?
Your health and fitness data is used only to calculate your macro targets and track progress within the app. We never share it with advertisers. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
The macros for a food look wrong. What do I do?
Email us at support@yallamacros.com with the food name and the value you believe is incorrect. Our database is actively curated and we update entries as feedback comes in.
A food I eat is missing from the database. Can you add it?
Yes — email us the name of the dish and, if possible, where it's from (e.g. "mansaf, Jordanian", "pancit canton, Filipino"). We prioritize regional dishes most commonly eaten in the UAE and the wider Gulf.
I'm having trouble signing in.
Try closing and reopening the app. If you signed up with email and password, make sure you're using the same email address. If the problem persists, email us with the email address you signed up with and a brief description of what you're seeing.
Is YallaMacros medical advice?
No. YallaMacros is an informational and tracking tool. It is not a substitute for advice from a registered dietitian, physician, or other qualified medical professional. If you have a medical condition or specific dietary needs, please consult a professional before changing your diet.

Still need help?

If your question isn't covered above, email support@yallamacros.com. Please include the email you signed up with and a short description of the issue. We aim to reply within 3 to 5 business days.
